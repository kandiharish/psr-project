## Animation Guidelines

Use only premium animations:

* Fade Up
* Fade Left
* Fade Right
* Scale In
* Stagger Reveal
* Parallax (very subtle)
* Smooth Scroll Effects

Avoid:

* Excessive movement
* Flashing effects
* Rotating elements
* Distracting animations

Every interaction should feel elegant, emotional, and respectful.
