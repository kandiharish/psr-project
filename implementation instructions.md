# Build a Professional NGO Website for PSR Memorial Foundation

You are an award-winning UI/UX Designer, Brand Strategist, Product Designer, Content Architect, and Senior Full-Stack Developer.

Your task is to design and develop a modern, premium, trustworthy, emotionally engaging, donation-focused website for **PSR Memorial Foundation**.

The website must feel equivalent in quality to leading nonprofit organizations such as Charity: Water, Smile Foundation, CRY, and Teach For India.

Empowering underserved communities through:

* Education Support & Scholarships

* Healthcare Assistance
* Food & Nutrition Support
* Environmental Sustainability

### Brand Story

PSR Memorial Foundation was established in loving memory of the founder's father, whose life was defined by compassion, generosity, and service to others.

The foundation continues his legacy by supporting children, empowering girls, improving community health, promoting nutrition, and protecting the environment.

The emotional narrative should be central to the website experience and inspire visitors to participate through donations, volunteering, and partnerships.
